Good to Know

Before You Paddle

🦺

Gear Provided

Life jackets (PFDs) and paddles are provided. Personal PFDs must meet USCG safety standards.

👕

Club Attire

Please wear your official Dragon Boat Club T-shirt to all training sessions. New members get a free club T-shirt!

📋

Waiver Required

All paddlers must complete a liability waiver before boarding. Sign it before your first session.

🔰

Safety First

Complete the safety briefing video before your first practice. Link above or on our website.
