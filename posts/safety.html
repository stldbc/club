Safety Guidelines

Please review these guidelines before every training session.
安全是我们共同的责任，每次训练前请仔细阅读。

🌤️

Pre-Training Safety Checks 训练前安全检查

  • ✅
    Weather Conditions – Check for extreme heat, storms, or high winds before heading out. Training should be canceled or modified if conditions are unsafe.
    天气状况 — 出发前请检查是否有高温、暴雨或大风等极端天气。如遇不安全天气，应取消或调整训练计划。
  • ✅
    Water Conditions – Assess the water current, visibility, and any debris that may pose a hazard.
    水域状况 — 评估水流速度、能见度以及是否有漂浮物或障碍物可能构成危险。
  • ✅
    Emergency Plan – Review emergency procedures, including nearest medical facilities and emergency contacts. Confirm team members with CPR experience; use the PulsePoint AED app to find nearby defibrillators.
    紧急预案 — 熟悉应急流程，包括最近的医疗设施和紧急联系人信息。确认队中有无具备 CPR 经验者，建议使用 PulsePoint AED 应用查找附近的自动体外除颤器。
  • ✅
    First Aid & Safety Equipment – Ensure a first aid kit, rescue throw rope, and life jackets are available and in good condition.
    急救与安全设备 — 确保现场配备急救包、救援抛绳和状况良好的救生衣。
🦺

Personal Safety Requirements 个人安全要求

  • ✅
    Life Jackets (PFDs) – All paddlers and team members must wear a properly fitted U.S. Coast Guard-approved personal flotation device at all times on the water.
    救生衣（PFD） — 所有划手及队员在水上训练期间必须全程穿戴合身的、通过美国海岸警卫队认证的救生衣。
  • ✅
    Hydration & Sun Protection – Bring water, wear sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat to prevent dehydration and sunburn.
    补水与防晒 — 请携带饮用水，使用防晒霜，佩戴太阳镜和帽子，防止脱水与晒伤。
  • ✅
    Proper Attire – Wear moisture-wicking, quick-drying clothes and closed-toe water shoes to prevent slipping.
    适当服装 — 建议穿着速干排汗衣物和包裹性强的水上鞋，防止打滑受伤。
  • ✅
    Health & Fitness Check – Participants should be in good health and free from conditions that could be worsened by intense physical activity.
    健康状况自检 — 参与者应身体健康，无严重影响剧烈运动的疾病或不适。
🐉

Boat & Equipment Safety Checks 龙舟与器材安全检查

  • ✅
    Boat Inspection – Check the hull for leaks or damage before launching.
    龙舟检查 — 下水前请检查船体是否有渗水或损伤。
  • ✅
    Paddle Condition – Inspect paddles for cracks or splinters that could cause injury.
    桨的状况 — 检查桨是否有裂纹或碎片，以免造成划伤。
  • ✅
    Seating & Weight Distribution – Ensure even weight distribution for balance and stability.
    座位与配重分布 — 确保船上人员分布均衡，保证稳定性与安全。
🚣

Training & Paddling Safety 训练与划行安全

  • ✅
    Coach & Steersperson Role – Follow all instructions from the coach and steersperson during training.
    教练与舵手职责 — 训练中请严格听从教练和舵手的指令。
  • ✅
    Proper Paddling Techniques – Use correct form to prevent strain or injury.
    正确划桨姿势 — 使用正确划桨方式，避免拉伤或其他运动伤害。
  • ✅
    Buddy System – Always have a partner assigned for accountability and assistance if needed.
    伙伴制度 — 每人应有一位"划船伙伴"，确保安全并在需要时能互相协助。
  • ✅
    Capsize & Recovery – Review and practice what to do if the boat tips over: stay calm, stay with the boat, and wait for assistance.
    翻船应对训练 — 学习并演练翻船时的处理方法，牢记：保持冷静，紧靠船体，等待救援。
  • ✅
    No Horseplay – Avoid unnecessary movements, standing, or leaning too far over the boat's edge.
    严禁打闹 — 禁止站立、剧烈晃动或探身至船边，避免发生意外。
🧘

Post-Training Safety 训练后安全措施

  • ✅
    Cool Down & Stretch – Prevent muscle soreness and injury with post-training stretching.
    拉伸与放松 — 训练结束后请做好拉伸运动，预防肌肉酸痛与受伤。
  • ✅
    Check for Injuries – Report any pain, blisters, or discomfort to the coach immediately.
    检查伤情 — 如有任何疼痛、水泡或不适，请立即报告教练或队医。
  • ✅
    Proper Equipment Storage – Clean and store paddles, PFDs, and the boat properly to maintain safety standards.
    器材整理 — 训练后请将桨、救生衣和龙舟清洁并妥善存放，保持设备良好状态。
⚠️

These guidelines are for informational purposes. All participants must complete a liability waiver before boarding. Contact us at stldragonboatclub@stlcls.org with any questions.

本指南仅供参考。所有参与者在上船前须签署免责声明。如有疑问请联系 stldragonboatclub@stlcls.org。
